Winchester Super Target delivers consistent performance and outstanding value for high-volume target shooting. Whether you're running clays at the trap field, cycling through skeet stations, or competing on a sporting clays course, Super Target loads give you the reliable, clean performance you need round after round.

The light target loads are optimized to minimize felt recoil without sacrificing pattern density, making them a favorite for extended shooting sessions and new shooters. Clean-burning powder keeps your barrel cleaner longer and reduces maintenance between sessions.

  • Round lead shot for consistent, repeatable downrange patterns
  • Hinged wad cushion reduces felt recoil for all-day comfort
  • Clean-burning powder and primer for less fouling
  • Consistent powder charge for uniform velocity and accuracy
  • Ideal for trap, skeet, and sporting clay competition
  • 25-round box — value priced for high-volume shooting
